Smoke Detector Service

Reliable battery replacement, testing, and proper placement for your family's safety

The Most Important Device You Never Think About

Smoke detectors save lives. But many homeowners install them and forget about them. Dead batteries are the most common reason home smoke detectors fail. Even worse, some homes have detectors in the wrong locations, reducing their effectiveness in a fire emergency.

A professional smoke detector service ensures your detectors are working reliably, properly positioned, and meet the 10-year replacement guidelines. We check battery status, test functionality, identify placement issues, and replace units that have reached end-of-life. It's simple maintenance that makes a real difference in protecting your family and home.

Proper Coverage for West Omaha Homes

Safety experts recommend smoke detectors on every level of your home, including the basement, plus outside each sleeping area and in the kitchen. Many West Omaha homes have gaps in coverage—hallway detectors but no bedroom protection, or kitchen units only without basement coverage.

We assess your home's layout and recommend strategic placement that maximizes life safety. If you have older detectors (over 10 years old), they may fail to detect smoke as effectively. We'll identify which units need replacement and help you upgrade safely and affordably.
