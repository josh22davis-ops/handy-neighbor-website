# Introduction to Membership — Document Spec

**Purpose:** A polished onboarding document sent to new members after signup that explains exactly what they're getting, how the service works, and what to expect. This replaces the need for the website to answer every operational question — it's the "welcome packet" that turns a subscriber into a confident, informed member.

**Format:** PDF (printable, email-attachable, professional). 4–6 pages. Branded with Handy Neighbor colors/fonts.

**When it's delivered:** After signup confirmation, before the first visit. Ideally emailed as a PDF with a short personal note from Josh.

---

## Document Outline

### Page 1: Welcome
- Personal welcome message from Josh
- "You're in. Here's everything you need to know."
- Quick summary: $88/month, 4 seasonal visits, all labor included, materials at cost, cancel anytime

### Page 2: What's Included — Full Service List
- All 29 included services with brief one-line descriptions grouped by category:
  - **Appliance Maintenance** (dryer vent, HVAC filters, fridge coils, dishwasher, washer, thermostat, condensate drain, humidifier pad, water softener)
  - **Safety & Inspections** (smoke/CO detectors, electrical check, sump pump, GFI outlets, garage door, rodent entry points)
  - **Home Systems & Seasonal** (hose bib winterization, leak checks, weatherstripping, drainage, downspout clearing, water/air quality, fireplace glass, home walkthrough, emergency lights, showerhead filter, ceiling fans, door hinges, cabinet hardware)
- Visual distinction between what happens every visit vs. what's seasonal/as-needed

### Page 3: Available Add-On Services
- All 8 add-ons with brief descriptions:
  - Gutter Cleaning, Pressure Washing, Sprinkler Startup, Sprinkler Blowout, Water Heater Flush, Gas Fireplace Inspection, Deck & Fence Staining, Permanent Holiday Lights
- How add-ons work: "Just ask during any visit or text/call between visits. We'll give you a flat-rate quote before any work starts."
- Clarify: add-ons are member-only pricing, scheduled alongside or between quarterly visits

### Page 4: How Your Membership Works
- **First Visit:** Home inspection walkthrough — inside and out. We document your home's systems, note what needs attention, and build your seasonal maintenance schedule. Typical duration: 60–90 minutes.
- **Quarterly Visits:** Each season has a tailored checklist based on your home. You'll get a text when your visit is coming up with a time window. Most visits are 2–3 hours depending on your home's needs.
- **Between Visits:** Text or call anytime with questions. If something comes up, we'll get it on the next visit or schedule a separate trip.
- **Materials:** When a filter, battery, or part needs replacing, we use quality materials and bill them separately at cost — no markup. You'll see an itemized list with every invoice.
- **Billing:** $88/month, charged on the same date each month. Cancel anytime — no fees, no contracts, no awkward conversations.

### Page 5: What to Expect on Your First Visit
- What to have ready (nothing complicated — just be home, point us to the furnace/water heater/etc.)
- What we'll check and document
- What you'll receive after: your custom seasonal maintenance schedule
- How long it takes
- "Don't clean up for us. We're here to see your home as it really is."

### Page 6: Contact & FAQ
- Josh's direct phone/text number
- Email
- Best times to reach out
- Quick FAQ:
  - "What if I need to reschedule?" → Just text, we'll find a new time.
  - "What if something breaks between visits?" → Text us. If it's in scope, we'll handle it at the next visit or schedule a trip.
  - "Can I pause instead of cancel?" → Yes, just let us know.
  - "Do I need to be home?" → For interior work, yes. Exterior-only visits can happen without you.

## Design Notes
- Match website branding: dark theme (#3B5068), gold accent (#C8A96E), cream text (#F5F0E8)
- Fonts: Bebas Neue for headers, Inter for body, Playfair Display for callouts
- Include the Handy Neighbor logo on every page
- Keep it clean and scannable — this isn't a legal document, it's a welcome packet
- Consider a "Your Home's Maintenance Calendar" visual showing what happens each season (Spring / Summer / Fall / Winter)

## Evolution Path
This document will evolve as the business grows:
- **v1 (now):** Static PDF, manually emailed after signup
- **v2 (web app):** Auto-generated with member's name, address, and custom maintenance schedule
- **v3 (platform):** Templated for franchise operators with territory-specific branding and service lists
