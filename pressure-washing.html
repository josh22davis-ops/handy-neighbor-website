Restore Your Home's Surfaces With Professional Pressure Washing

Over time, your home's exterior surfaces accumulate dirt, mold, algae, and weathering that dulls your curb appeal and creates safety hazards. A grimy driveway isn't just unattractive—slippery algae and moss can make walkways dangerous for family and guests. Pressure washing removes years of buildup in a single service, restoring your surfaces to like-new condition.

The Handy Neighbor Co. uses professional-grade equipment and techniques to clean driveways, sidewalks, patios, and siding safely and effectively. We're careful to apply the right pressure and approach for each surface—too much power can damage delicate finishes, so our experience matters. Whether you're preparing for a gathering, increasing your home's market appeal, or simply want surfaces that look great, we deliver the results West Omaha homeowners expect.

Serving The Ridges, Shadow Ridge, Armbrust Acres, Bay Shores, Leawood, and Pacific Park for over 20 years, we know what West Omaha homes need. Josh Davis, our owner-operator, is licensed and insured—and brings the personal touch that makes the difference when working around your property.
