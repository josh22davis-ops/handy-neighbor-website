Seasonal Gutter Maintenance You Can Trust

Your gutters are your home's first line of defense against water damage. Over time, leaves, debris, and sediment accumulate in gutters and downspouts, preventing proper drainage and directing water toward your foundation. In West Omaha, where we experience heavy spring rains and autumn leaf fall, regular gutter maintenance isn't optional—it's essential.

The Handy Neighbor Co. offers professional gutter cleaning services designed to keep your drainage system flowing smoothly. We clear gutters of debris, inspect for damage, and ensure downspouts are directing water safely away from your foundation. Whether you need spring cleanup after winter, or fall preparation before heavy rains, we're here to keep your gutters in top shape.

With over 20 years of experience serving homeowners across The Ridges, Shadow Ridge, Armbrust Acres, Bay Shores, Leawood, and Pacific Park, we understand the specific challenges West Omaha homes face. Our owner-operated service means you're working directly with a licensed, insured professional who treats your home like his own.

What's Included in Our Gutter Cleaning

  • Complete gutter cleaning—removing leaves, debris, and sediment
  • Downspout clearing and flush testing
  • Visual inspection for damage, gaps, or separation
  • Downspout extension verification—ensuring water flows away from foundation
  • Detailed notes on any repairs recommended
  • Professional cleanup of debris from your property

We focus on clearing the gutters and checking for clogs—not full replacements. If we spot damage or issues that need professional attention, we'll let you know so you can plan ahead.
