About Your Neighbor

Josh Davis — Owner & Operator

The Handy Neighbor Co. started the way most good ideas do — a neighbor needed help with something small, and I was the guy who actually showed up.

I'm a semi-retired contractor with over two decades of hands-on experience across residential construction and commercial facility management. I've done everything from framing and roofing to finish trim and shower door installs. Closets, mirrors, bath accessories, LVP flooring, windows, siding, light electrical, plumbing — if it's in a house, I've probably built it, fixed it, or replaced it.

As a Facility Manager, I ran maintenance on full mechanical systems — HVAC units, commercial kitchen equipment, water heaters, garage doors — the kind of work that teaches you how buildings actually work, not just how they look.

Now I'm channeling all of that into something simpler: helping my neighbors keep their homes in great shape without the hassle of hiring a big contractor for small jobs. I run the routes myself, I bring my own tools, and I treat every home like it's on my street — because it probably is.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a handyman cost in Shadow Ridge, Omaha? +

Flat rates between $50 and $300 for service day visits. Lightbulb replacement is $50–$100, filter changes $50–$100, gutter cleaning $100–$200, smoke detectors $50–$100, caulking $75–$150, and pressure washing $150–$300. Small projects start at $150 for the first hour ($89/hr after), and full-day contractor work is $750/day. No hidden fees.

Is there a handyman near Shadow Ridge or The Ridges? +

Yes. The Handy Neighbor Co. is owned and operated by Josh Davis, a resident of Shadow Ridge with 20+ years of construction experience. Route-based service days run through Shadow Ridge, The Ridges, Elkhorn, and surrounding West Omaha neighborhoods. Because Josh lives in the neighborhood, travel time is minimal and scheduling is flexible.

What services does The Handy Neighbor Co. offer? +

Route-based service days include lightbulb replacement, HVAC and fridge filter changes, gutter cleaning, smoke detector and CO detector maintenance, caulking and weatherstripping, and pressure washing. Beyond service days, we handle deck repairs, fence repairs, trim carpentry, drywall, bathroom refreshes, siding, and more. We also offer an Annual Home Checkup for $199–$299.

What areas do you serve? +

We currently serve Shadow Ridge, The Ridges, Elkhorn, West Omaha, Millard, Papillion, La Vista, Gretna, and Ralston. We're expanding to new neighborhoods based on demand — if your area isn't listed, book anyway and we'll prioritize neighborhoods with the most requests.

How do route-based service days work? +

Service days are themed around one type of job — like Lightbulb Day, Gutter Clean Day, or Filter Day. You pick the service you need and book online. We schedule you into an efficient neighborhood route, give you a time window, and send a heads-up when we're on the way. Most jobs are done in 15–30 minutes at a flat rate with no surprises.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes. The Handy Neighbor Co. is licensed and insured. Owner Josh Davis has over 20 years of experience in residential construction and commercial facility management, including framing, roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and finish carpentry.

How often should I clean my gutters in Omaha? +

At least twice a year — once in late spring after cottonwood season, and once in late fall after leaves drop. Homes in The Ridges and Elkhorn with mature trees may need more frequent cleaning. Our Gutter Clean Day runs $100–$200 and includes downspout flush and visual roof inspection.

How much does pressure washing cost in Omaha? +

We offer pressure washing for a flat rate of $150–$300, covering driveways, sidewalks, patios, and siding. This is typically lower than standalone pressure washing companies because of our route-based model — multiple homes in the same neighborhood are serviced the same day, reducing overhead.
