Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a “service day”? +

A service day is a scheduled route through the neighborhood focused on one type of job — like Lightbulb Day, Filter Day, or Gutter Clean Day. You book the service you need, get a time window, and the truck rolls through your area. Most jobs are done in 15–30 minutes at a flat rate. No estimates, no surprises.

How much does it cost? +

Service day visits run $50–$150 depending on the job. Lightbulbs and filters are $50–$100, gutters $100–$150, caulking $75–$150, and pressure washing $100–$150. Every price is flat-rate — you know exactly what it costs before the work starts. No hourly guessing, no hidden fees.

Who does the work? +

Josh Davis — owner, operator, and the only person who shows up. Licensed and insured with 20+ years of hands-on experience in residential construction and commercial facility management. No subcontractors. No strangers. Just a neighbor with the right tools and the know-how to use them.

What neighborhoods are currently served? +

Routes currently run through Shadow Ridge, The Ridges, Elkhorn, West Omaha, Millard, Papillion, La Vista, Gretna, and Ralston. New neighborhoods are added based on demand — if your area isn’t listed, book anyway. Areas with the most requests get added first.

Do I need to be home? +

Depends on the job. Exterior work like gutter cleaning and pressure washing can usually be done without anyone home. Interior jobs need someone there to let the truck in. Either way, you’ll get a time window and a heads-up notification when the route is nearby.

What if the job takes longer than expected? +

The price you see is the price you pay. Flat-rate means flat-rate — whether the job takes 15 minutes or 45. If something unexpected comes up during a visit, you’ll be told before any extra work happens. No surprises, ever.

What about bigger projects? +

The Handy Neighbor Co. also handles small projects, repairs, and contractor-level work beyond the regular service days. Deck repairs, fence work, drywall, trim carpentry, bathroom refreshes — if it’s in a house, it’s probably been built, fixed, or replaced before. Reach out to talk through what you need.

How do I book? +

Pick a service, fill in the short form below, and submit. A confirmation with your service day and time window comes back within 24 hours. You can also call or text (402) 350-2583 or email hello@thehandyneighbor.co.
