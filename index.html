What Gets Handled

Highlighted Home Services

These are the jobs that protect your home, save you money long-term, and quietly fall off the list every season. Every membership plan starts here.

Clean Air & Clean Water

Essentials+

HVAC filters, refrigerator water filters, refrigerator air filters, and showerhead filters — your home is full of filters working to keep your air clean and your water safe. When they fall behind, air quality drops and water picks up what it shouldn’t. Regular replacement keeps your family breathing and drinking clean.

Appliance Maintenance

Premium

Ever wonder why your dishwasher smells? It’s the lift pump filter — and almost nobody knows to clean it. Sprayer flush, filter cleaning, and a full maintenance cycle get your dishes actually clean again. Front-load washers have the same problem — that musty smell is usually the drain pump filter sitting full of gunk. Same visit covers dryer vent cleaning and hose checks.

Fireplace Maintenance

Premium

When’s the last time you could actually see the flames? Most homeowners don’t realize how much better their fireplace looks until the glass is pulled and cleaned. That hazy film builds up slowly — then one cleaning and it’s like a brand new unit. Full service includes glass cleaning, inspection, and seasonal startup.

Water & Air Testing

Plus+

When’s the last time you tested your water for chlorine, pH, lead, hardness, or nitrates? Or checked your indoor air for humidity, CO2, particulates, or VOCs? Most families have no idea what they’re breathing or drinking. Routine testing gives you real numbers — and a clear picture of what your home actually needs.

Sprinkler Blowout & Spring Setup

Add-On Service

Fall blowout. Spring startup. The two appointments every Omaha homeowner scrambles to book. Handled automatically as part of your plan — no phone calls, no forgetting, no frozen lines.

Downspouts & Drainage

Plus+

Clogged downspouts and window wells push water toward your foundation — the most expensive part of any home to repair. Seasonal clearing reduces the likelihood of water damage and costly foundation issues.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I sign up? +

A walkthrough of your home — inside and out. Filters, seals, safety systems, drainage, water and air quality testing, weather prep, and anything else that needs regular attention. From there, a custom maintenance plan is built with clear pricing. No pressure, no obligation.

How much does the membership cost? +

Our three-tier membership packages are $45, $75, and $125 per month. Each package is then tailored to your home’s needs. Every price is flat-rate. No hourly guessing, no hidden fees.

Who does the work? +

Josh Davis — owner, operator, and the only person who shows up. Licensed and insured with 20+ years of hands-on experience in residential construction and commercial facility management. No subcontractors. No strangers. Just a neighbor with the right tools and the know-how to use them.

What neighborhoods are currently served? +

Routes currently run through The Ridges, Shadow Ridge, Armbrust Acres, Bay Shores, Leawood, and Pacific Park in West Omaha. New neighborhoods are added based on demand — if your area isn’t listed, sign up anyway. Areas with the most interest get added first.

Do I need to be home during visits? +

Yes — someone needs to be home or leave a way for me to access the house. Depending on the services scheduled, some light prep may be needed ahead of time. For example, the dishwasher should be emptied before a dishwasher service. You’ll always get a heads-up on what to expect before each visit.

What if something unexpected comes up? +

Your membership service price never changes. But sometimes during a visit, I’ll discover something bigger — a part that’s too worn for maintenance to help, or a surprise that needs real repair work. When that happens, I’ll let you know exactly what I found, give you an estimate or recommendation, and we’ll figure out the best path forward together. You’re a member, and I treat your home the way I’d treat my own.

What about bigger projects? +

I do take on small projects outside of the quarterly plan — bathroom refreshes, whole-house fixture updates, deck work, siding and roofing repairs, drainage, and more. That said, my priority is the quarterly membership service, so I’m selective about how much project work I take on. Members always get first priority for small projects before I open anything up to non-members. Reach out to talk through what you need.

How do I get started? +

Fill out the short form below to get your custom maintenance plan. You’ll get a confirmation within 24 hours. After a walkthrough, a plan is built for your home — and you decide if it’s the right fit. You can also call or text (402) 350-2583 or email hello@thehandyneighbor.co.
