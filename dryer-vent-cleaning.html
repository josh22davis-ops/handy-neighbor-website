Protect Your Home From Dryer Fire Risk

Dryer lint buildup is one of the leading causes of home fires in the United States. As lint accumulates in your vent system, it restricts airflow, forcing your dryer to work harder and generating dangerous heat buildup. Many homeowners don't realize their dryer vents need professional cleaning—they assume emptying the lint trap is enough. It's not.

Over time, lint gets past the trap and builds up deeper in your venting system, in places you can't reach with basic cleaning. This hidden lint creates a serious fire hazard and makes your dryer work less efficiently, driving up energy costs and shortening your dryer's lifespan. The Handy Neighbor Co. provides professional dryer vent cleaning to remove that dangerous buildup and restore your dryer to safe, efficient operation.
